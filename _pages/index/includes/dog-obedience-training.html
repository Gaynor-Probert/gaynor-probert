Dog Obedience Training

Puppy Socialisation classes - Gaynor Probert will teach you, the most important aspects of your dog's education and how to develop him to be a well behaved and balanced pet you have always wanted. Clicker training is one of the ways she achives this, it is good fun and all the family can join in.

Bronze Obedience Course - How to teach your dog to walk on a slack lead without pulling and to come back when called are the two prioritys in this course we also train you how to prevent your dog barging through the door when you are going out.

Silver Obedience Course - In this course you learn how to train your dog to go at your pace and to come back to you when there are distractions around.You will be taught how to get your dog to stay when told. It is in this course that we start the agility training and the dogs love this activity.

Gold Obedience Course - In this course you learn how to get your dog to walk by your side off lead and come back to your side when you are walking. We add another interest, how to get your dog to find his toy when it is amongst others, The agility continues for your dog to enjoy.

Platinum Obedience Course - Changing pace while weaving through people and their dogs. Stay while you go out of sight. Distance control and emergancy down are taught in this course and the agility is now done off lead as now you have full control of your dog.

One to One - A one to one with Gaynor Probert offers you a unique oppertunity to expand your understanding of your dog and to improve the obedience that you are having problems with.
